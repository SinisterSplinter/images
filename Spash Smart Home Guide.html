Custom Commands
"Weather Report"
Provide today's temperature and chance of rain.
"Night Lights"
Enable low-light scene on the first floor.
"Vacuum First/Second Floor"
Vacuum the corresponding floor for 30 minutes.
"Start/End House Vacuum”
Start/End vacuuming on both floors.
“Enable Vacation Mode”
Turn off all lights, enable Away Lighting, and set thermostat to 65°–75° heat/cool.
